char32_t_to_UTF8 converts internal 32 bit codepoints to UTF8.

Parameters
sourceis a 32 bit codepoint
targetis a 5 unsigned byte buffer from the caller.

The codepoint is converted to UTF8 with trailing NUL bytes such that it is immediately ready for output via std::cout.

Branch points are avoided. Maximum cost is 23 Intel opcodes.
